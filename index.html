Meet Julie

A different kind of styling

Every mother wants to feel her best at her daughter's wedding. But for most women, finding something to wear is stressful, confusing, and quietly lonely. Browsing alone, trying on dresses that don't feel right, settling for something safe because no one helped them understand what would actually work.


Julie works inside a bridal boutique, which means she meets mothers of the bride at the exact moment they're beginning to think about their own look. She sees the uncertainty firsthand. She understands the emotions. And she's built a private styling practice around guiding each mother through a clear, personal process that ends with real confidence.
